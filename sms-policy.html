Program

Zoab Painting may send SMS/MMS texts for painting estimates, appointments, project updates, customer service, and—only with separate consent—marketing/reactivation.

How to opt in

  • Website estimate/contact form with separate service and marketing checkboxes (unchecked by default), or
  • Verbal/written request for service texts when you provide your mobile number for a job/estimate, or
  • Texting our business first about services.

Example form: SMS opt-in page.

Frequency & cost

Message frequency varies (low volume). Message and data rates may apply.

STOP = opt out · HELP = help · Consent is not a condition of purchase.

Privacy

Mobile information is not sold or shared with third parties for their own marketing. Messaging providers process texts on our behalf. See Privacy Policy and Terms.
