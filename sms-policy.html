SMS/Text Messaging Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

What Is This?

Zoab Painting ("we," "us," "our") may send text messages (SMS/MMS) to customers who have provided their phone number and requested our services.

How You Opt In

You consent to receive text messages from Zoab Painting when you:

  • Call our business phone number and provide your contact information to request an estimate or service
  • Submit a contact form or estimate request on our website and provide your phone number
  • Text our business number first to inquire about services
  • Verbally provide your phone number to a Zoab Painting representative and request follow-up

By providing your phone number through any of these methods and requesting service, you expressly consent to receive text messages from Zoab Painting related to your inquiry, estimate, scheduled work, and service follow-ups.

What Messages You Will Receive

Messages may include:

  • Follow-ups regarding your painting estimate or quote
  • Appointment and scheduling confirmations or reminders
  • Job status updates
  • Post-job satisfaction check-ins
  • Occasional service reactivation outreach

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You will typically receive 1-5 messages per month depending on your active service status. This is a low-volume messaging program.

How to Opt Out

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any text message.

After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further texts will be sent.

To re-subscribe, reply START at any time.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or call us at (515) 705-6250.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply. Zoab Painting does not charge for text messages, but your wireless carrier's standard messaging rates may apply.

Privacy

Your phone number and personal information will not be shared with third parties for marketing purposes. See our full Privacy Policy.

Supported Carriers

Our messaging service is compatible with all major US wireless carriers including AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon, Sprint, and others.
