SMS/Text Messaging Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

ZOAB LLC, doing business as Zoab Painting (“Zoab Painting,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), may send SMS/MMS text messages to mobile numbers when you consent as described below.

How you opt in — service / transactional texts

You agree to receive service-related text messages when you:

  • Submit our website estimate/contact form and check the service SMS consent box (unchecked by default), or
  • Provide your mobile number by phone or in person when requesting an estimate or service and receive clear notice that you may get service-related texts, with the right to reply STOP anytime, or
  • Text our business number first to inquire about services (we may continue that conversation by text).

Service texts may include: estimate responses; appointment confirmations and reminders; project scheduling and status updates; customer follow-up related to your job; invoice or payment links; and customer-service conversations.

How you opt in — marketing texts

Promotional or reactivation texts are sent only if you give separate marketing consent (for example, an optional marketing checkbox that is unchecked by default). Requesting an estimate or agreeing to service texts does not by itself authorize marketing texts.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies. This is a low-volume messaging program (typically a small number of messages per week while a project is active; fewer when inactive).

Cost

Message and data rates may apply. Zoab Painting does not charge a separate fee for SMS; your wireless carrier’s rates may apply.

Opt out

Reply STOP to any Zoab Painting text to opt out. You may receive one confirmation of your opt-out. Reply START to resubscribe to service updates where that option is offered.

Help

Reply HELP for help, call (515) 414-8441, or email zoabpainting@gmail.com.

Consent is not a condition of purchase

Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of purchase and is not required to receive an estimate.

Privacy

We do not sell your mobile number to third parties for their own marketing. Mobile information will not be sold or shared with third parties for their own marketing or promotional purposes. Messaging providers (for example Twilio and carrier partners) may process texts and numbers on our behalf solely to deliver messages and maintain compliance records. Messaging providers may process texts on our behalf. See our Privacy Policy.

Carriers

Compatible with major U.S. carriers. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
