This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect personal information when you use our website, request an estimate, hire us, or communicate with us by phone, email, or SMS/MMS text message.

3. How we use information

4. SMS / text messaging (mobile information)

If you provide a mobile number and consent, we may send SMS/MMS messages as described here and in our Terms and SMS Policy.

  • Service/transactional texts require service SMS consent and may include estimate responses, appointment confirmations and reminders, project scheduling and status, job-related follow-up, invoice or payment links, and customer-service conversations.
  • Marketing texts require separate marketing consent (for example a separate unchecked checkbox). Service consent alone is not marketing consent.
  • Message frequency varies. This is a low-volume program.
  • Message and data rates may apply. Zoab Painting does not charge a separate SMS fee; your carrier may.
  • Opt out: Reply STOP to any Zoab Painting text to cancel. You may receive one opt-out confirmation. Reply START to resubscribe where offered.
  • Help: Reply HELP, call (515) 414-8441, or email zoabpainting@gmail.com.
  • Consent is not a condition of purchase and is not required to receive an estimate.

Mobile information will not be sold or shared with third parties for their own marketing or promotional purposes.

We may share mobile numbers and message content with service providers who process communications on our behalf (for example our SMS platform / carrier partners such as Twilio and related aggregators) solely to deliver messages, maintain consent/opt-out records, prevent abuse, and operate the messaging program. Those providers may not use your mobile information to market their own products to you.

Opt-in page (example of website consent): SMS opt-in / estimate form.
